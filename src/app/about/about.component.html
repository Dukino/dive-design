There has been a neighborhood bar on the corner of Sixth and Salem since prohibition, with evidence of a basement speakeasy. So when the opportunity to revitalize the bar at 827 N. Sixth St. presented itself we jumped at the idea. With a lineage that includes: Klippel's Tavern, Warwick's Tavern, The Squirrel Cage, The Clubhouse, and the Varsity Clubhouse, we are proud to give you the "Sixth Street Dive."

With strong roots in Texas, we offer Lafayette a taste of home with authentic Tex-Mex food available everyday from 11am - 3am and an atmosphere that would fit perfectly on the famed 6th Street in Austin TX.

Out of respect to the bar and neighborhood's history we also offer many "Hoosier Favorites." Quirky and weird, we are doing things a little different by showcasing things we've experienced at our favorite spots all over the country!
